  • Orange
  • DancintoTheBlues

One of Frank Ocean's lyrics and his famous debut album, Channel Orange, helped inspire this poem. I wrote it when I was going through a tough time with one of my best friends. It had to do with a girl and caused a lot of self-reflection that led to a realization that our ties and issues were.... "Earthly".

  • I hope you stand a little taller
  • So you can see over the clouds
  • And not drown
  • In this Earthly bullshit

  • Our stitches seemed entangled
  • The way we breathe
  • Each other's names
  • Over sinful fallacies

  • Our yellow brick road
  • Has come to a split
  • Where we must untether souls
  • And walk freely

  • I hope you know I was never proud
  • If I could flee I would fly home South
  • But all I do is dream of gravity
  • Keeping us around.

  • A poem by, Marcos Bravo
