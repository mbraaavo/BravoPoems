  • DancintoTheBlues

My inspiration for this poem was retribution and finding my true self again. Ultimately going through an existential crisis, only to end up discovering my identity as a creative man and more importantly, a human.

  • Burnt Gardens
  • Full of priceless daises.
  • I searched
  • Through a thousand ashes
  • Collecting petals and
  • Stuffing them in my pockets
  • Till my skin turned grey
  • And my heart sang
  • Intermingled shades of yellow.

  • A poem by, Marcos Bravo
