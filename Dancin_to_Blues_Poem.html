DancintoTheBlues was a poem inspired by lonliness and a Tyler the Creator the lyric. I wrote this poem shortly after pulling myself out of a depressive state, but I still had a lingering feeling of sadness. The poem expresses my feelings of being unheard and how the feeling followed me wherever I went.

  • And my lips were left unzippedas
  • The drool dripped
  • Into a pool of unspoken wordswhere
  • My ego slipped and drowned.

  • The blues were acrylic&

  • Every step I took
  • The road was painted thick,
